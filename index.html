PROJECT OVERVIEW/GOAL

The Lifestyle Snapshot Dashboard project is a comprehensive analysis conducted entirely in Microsoft Excel. It aims to illuminate the public interest landscape in 2024. By analyzing a comprehensive survey dataset, the project seeks to uncover the predominant preferences, hobbies, and emerging trends shaping societal dynamics.

ADDRESSED CHALLENGES
  • Data Complexity: Analyzing diverse demographic and preference data demanded advanced analytical techniques.
  • Demographic Segmentation: Segmenting the dataset by age, gender, and lifestyle preferences for meaningful insights.
  • Trend Identification: Identifying significant trends in leisure activities, travel preferences, and lifestyle choices within the extensive dataset.
DATASET SUMMARY

The Lifestyle Snapshot Dashboard project is based on a comprehensive survey dataset collected to explore the pulse of public interest in 2024. This dataset, hosted on Kaggle, encompasses a diverse range of topics, shedding light on the preferences and trends that define the current cultural and social landscape. You can access the dataset here.

UTILIZED SKILLS
  • Data Cleaning & Transformation: Leveraged Power Query in Excel for efficient data cleaning and transformation processes.
  • Data Modeling & Analysis: Utilized Pivot Tables in Excel for comprehensive data modeling and analysis.
  • Data Visualization: Employed Pivot Charts in Excel to create visually appealing and informative data visualizations.
  • Statistical Analysis: Applied statistical methods within Excel for uncovering insights and correlations in the dataset.
  • Demographic Segmentation: Utilized Excel to segment data based on demographic variables like age, gender, and lifestyle preferences.
  • Trend Forecasting: Utilized Excel to combine data-driven insights and forecast emerging trends in public interests.
  • Critical Thinking: Applied critical thinking skills in Excel to interpret data and discern meaningful trends.
KEY INSIGHTS
  • Age Overview: Majority of individuals fall within the age range of 19-21.
  • Gender Ratio: 84% Male, 16% Female.
  • Leisure Breakdown: Watching Movies and Playing Outdoor Games are popular activities.
  • Travelers Analysis: 89% individuals are travelers.
  • Travel Vehicles: Cars are the most preferred mode of travel.
  • Sleep Comfort: 85.25% individuals find their sleep comfortable.
  • Comfortable Lifestyle: 85.25% individuals lead a comfortable lifestyle.
  • Social Preferences: 83.61% prefer family over friends.
  • Family Size Trend: Majority have a family size of 2-4.
  • Favorite Food Preferences: Male individuals prefer Nonveg Meals and Veg Meals.
  • Cooking Skills: 77% individuals have cooking skills.
  • Preferred Seasons: Winter is the most preferred season.
  • Favorite Organisms: Domestic Animals are the most favored.
